Choosing projects

19 September '24

Here it goes, my first blog post is official 😁!

Today we had an info session about the projects we can choose from in our the first semester. The big theme of most of the projects is Smart Greenhouse-how can we make a greenhouse self sustainable? Some examples are: greenhouse water system, greenhouse energy, greenhouse sensoring, ect.

There are also two other projects that are not related to the Smart Greenhouse theme. One of them is a project about smart cameras to grow watercress by collecting data and make an AI model to predict the growth of the watercress.

The other project involves making CoMoveIT, an intelligent wheelchair steering system, compatible with Linx motor-steering-based wheelchairs. This project is in collaboration with the company CoMoveIT. I will put a link to their website down below if you want to check it out.

So as you can see, there are a lot of interesting projects to choose from. We have to send our top 3 choices to the school by the end of the week. I haven't read all the details about every project yet, so thats the plan for this week. I've set a personal deadline to have my top 3 choices by Thursday evening.

For now I'll end this post by sharing my top 3 choices at the moment:

  1. Watercress AI
  2. CoMoveIT interface
  3. Greenhouse sensoring
