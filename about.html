Who is Lynn?


I'm Lynn Delaere, I'm year's old and i'm currently a student at VIVES University of Applied Sciences in Bruges where I study Electronics-ICT.


In 2015, I've graduated as a vet nurse at VIVES University of Applied Sciences in Roeselare. After my studies, I started working as a shop assistant in various retail business. Working in retail was a great experience for me, I learned a lot about customer service and how to work in a team.

After a few years in retail I wanted to try something new and started working as a field technician at Anticimex. I've learned a lot about pest control and had the opportunity to work with a many different people.


But after a while I noticed that I wasn't happy with my job anymore. I wanted to do something more challenging, something that would make truly happy. In 2023, with the support of my friends and family I decided to quit my job and go back to school.


I've always been interested in technology and computers and I wanted to learn more about it. Now a year later, I'm in my second year Electronics-ICT and I'm still happy with my decision. I'm learning new things every day and I'm excited to see what the future will bring.


My goal is to not only to improve my skills but also to inspire others to follow their dreams and do what makes them happy.
